About Me

I am a 3rd-year PhD student in the Department of Biostatistics at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health. I currently work with Dr. Ingo Ruczinski and Dr. H. Benjamin Larman to characterize antibody responses to HIV and phage components of the human microbiome.

Developing Cross-Sectional Methods to Estimate HIV Incidence

HIV incidence can be determined by following cohorts of HIV-uninfected individuals and quantifying the number of new HIV cases or by using cross-sectional methods. Current cross-sectional methods of estimating HIV incidence utilize multi-assay algorithms (MAAs) to classify individuals as recently or non-recently infected.
