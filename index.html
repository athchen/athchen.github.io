Athena Chen

PhD Candidate in Biostatistics

I am a 5th-year PhD student in the Department of Biostatistics at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health. I currently work with Dr. Ingo Ruczinski and Dr. H. Benjamin Larman to characterize antibody responses to various antigens using phage-immunoprecipitation sequencing technology (PhIP-Seq). My research interests include Bayesian statistics, statistical genomics and proteomics, data analysis and data analysis teaching, immunology and the human microbiome.
